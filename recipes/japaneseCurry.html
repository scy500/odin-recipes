Japanese curry

Description

Delicious japanese curry! Brought to japan by indians, enhanced with japanese taste ! Can be cooked with shrimps, chicken, beef or pork. Yummy !

Ingredients

  • 500g Meat or fish
  • Rice
  • One apple
  • 250g carrots
  • Potatoes
  • 2 onions
  • Garlic
  • Ketchup
  • Honey
  • Soy sauce
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Curry Roux ( e.g Golden Curry )

Steps

  1. Start cutting your meat and salt & pepper it
  2. Start by frying your meat and onions in some oil and garlic
  3. If the meat changes colour, start adding 1,2L water
  4. Start cutting and washing the carrots and potatoes
  5. If the water is boiling, put in the carrots
  6. Peel the apple and then grate it into the pot
  7. Next add the potatoes
  8. You can start cooking the rice now
  9. Let it boil for another 20 minutes, until the potatoes are done
  10. Add two or three big spoons of honey and soy sauce, and some ketchup
  11. Prepare a second pot and add 3 or 4 blocks of roux and mix it with some water from the other pot
  12. Stir it for about 2 minutes until the roux mixed with the water, then put it back into the other pot and stir it good
  13. The longer you let it boil now, the thicker it gets. If you dont want to wait you can add either wheat flour or cornstarch

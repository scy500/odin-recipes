Garlic Shrimp Spaghetti with olive oil

Description

A delicious dish with garlic, olive oil shrimps and Spaghetti. Easy and quick to make but still delicious!

Ingredients

  • 500g Spaghetti
  • 200g shrimps
  • Fresh garlic
  • Salt
  • 12 spoons olive oil
  • A bunch of parsley

Steps

  1. Start cooking the spaghetti, add some salt into the water
  2. While cooking the spaghetti, fry the shrimps in olive oil and add 2 cloves of garlic
  3. After 10 minutes the spaghetti are done, add them now into your fry pan with the shrimps
  4. Stir it some and let it cook for a few minutes
  5. After its done, you can cut some parsley and add it on top of the dish
